Password Generator

Password Length
Password length is crucial for security, with 12-16 characters recommended for strength. Longer passwords with mixed characters increases the security and safety of the password.
Include UpperCase
Including uppercase letters in passwords enhances security by adding complexity and making them harder to crack.
Include LowerCase
Including lowercase letters in passwords enhances security by adding complexity and making them harder to crack.
Include Numbers
Including numbers in passwords strengthens security by adding diversity and increasing complexity.
Include Symbols
Including symbols in passwords enhances security by adding unique characters and increasing complexity. A strong password blends symbols, uppercase, lowercase letters, and numbers to maximize protection. Passwords without symbols are easier to crack and less resistant to attacks.
