PassFort Guide

Context

PassFort is a secure password manager that helps you store, manage, and quickly access your passwords in a safe, encrypted environment.

Add a Password

  1. Click Add Password in the sidebar.
  2. Enter the website URL, username, your desired password, a 4-digit safety key, and an optional note.
  3. Click Save to encrypt and store your entry.

Manage Passwords

  1. Select Password Manager from the sidebar to view stored entries.
  2. Click a row to display details in the panel on the right.
  3. Enter your safety key and click Show to decrypt your password.
  4. Use the copy buttons to copy the username or password, or click the globe icon to visit the website.
  5. Clicked the trash icon to delete an entry permanently.

Security

All passwords are encrypted with AES-256 and require your safety key for decryption. Keep your safety key private and secure.
